About MPHS Fairview

Mother of Perpetual Help School, Inc. (MPHS Fairview) is a nonprofit K–12 institution along Iris Street, Dahlia Avenue in Quezon City. Since 1990, we have guided Perpetualites with the motto Humility in Greatness — blending Catholic values, academic rigor, and community spirit.

Our official Facebook page shares enrollment updates, class schedules, SSLG activities, and weather advisories for families. Follow us for the latest school news and events.
