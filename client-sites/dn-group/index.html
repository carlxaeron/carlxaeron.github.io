Our story

Built since 1983

DN Steel Marketing, Incorporated began as a modest trading company on Commonwealth Avenue, Quezon City — and grew into a trusted manufacturing and marketing partner for the Philippine building industry.

In 1992, DN Steel started importing its own pre-painted coils and rolls, creating distinct DN profiles. Today the DN Group of Companies delivers roofing, metals, insulation, and specialty systems through specialized affiliates.

We meet expectations with craftsmanship and construction that stand the test of time.
