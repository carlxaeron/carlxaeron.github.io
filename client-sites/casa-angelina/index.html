About Casa Angelina

Casa Angelina Resort is a private vacation home in Congress Village, Manggahan — a quiet getaway just outside the city with a lit pool, karaoke lounge, and dining pavilion built for family time.

With 6.5K Facebook followers and an 88% recommend rating, guests keep coming back for reunions, birthdays, and barkada weekends.
