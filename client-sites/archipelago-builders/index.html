Our story

Building since 1965

Established as Archipelago Builders in 1965 — growing from a rented room on Rizal Avenue, Sta. Cruz, Manila to its own headquarters on North Avenue, Quezon City.

From Category C to Category AAA, the firm earned its standing through expanded operations for both public and private sectors across Metro Manila, Southern Luzon, and the Visayas.

Horizontal and vertical. One contractor. Six decades of delivery.
