About Costa Abril

Costa Abril Resort is a water park in San Jose, Rodriguez (Montalban), Rizal, built around its wave pool, giant slide, and kiddie slide. With over 104K Facebook followers, it's a well-known day-out destination for families and groups across Rizal and Metro Manila.
